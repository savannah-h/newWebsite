Savannah Bryn Halvorson

Hi! My name is Savannah. I’m 17 years old and a senior at Santa Catalina School in Monterey, although I live in Modesto.

I’m interested in art, puzzles, and coding. I enjoy drawing, sketching, and painting with traditional mediums, with a strong focus on stylized humans. My most common hobby is playing games online, especially those of a puzzle or murder-mystery variety, like chess or Ace Attorney. I’m currently learning how to code, and can program in both Processing and Python with competency. I'm also learning HTML at the moment, which is how I made this website! (That and Google...)

While my time at school began my interest in programming, it also spurred a few of my other activities as well. For instance, I’ll be taking my fourth year of French classes, which I have been very passionate about due to the friends I’ve made online who reside in French-speaking areas. I also love theater tech, working backstage on sets or costumes to make a play run perfectly without ever having to step onstage.

I easily adapt to all situations, get very invested in whatever I’m currently doing, and have a drive to succeed. No matter where I end up, I am determined to be a valuable asset.

Please hire me.
